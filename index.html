Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI narration?

Our AI narration is highly accurate, with over 98% pronunciation accuracy for standard English text. The system handles complex words, proper nouns, and various dialects with impressive precision. For specialized content like scientific or technical material, you can also customize pronunciations.

Can I use EchoRead for commercial purposes?

Yes, our service includes licenses for commercial use. This means you can use our service to create audiobooks for sale, podcasts, educational content, or any other commercial application. For large-scale commercial usage, please contact our sales team for custom enterprise solutions.

What file formats do you support for the output?

EchoRead generates audiobooks in MP3, WAV, and M4B formats. We also offer AAC format with chapter markers for enhanced navigation. All audio files are optimized for quality and file size, making them perfect for mobile listening.

How long does it take to convert an eBook?

Conversion time depends on the length of your eBook. As a general guideline, a 300-page novel (approximately 80,000 words) takes about 10-15 minutes to convert. Our system processes multiple books simultaneously, so you can queue up several conversions at once without additional waiting time.

Can I customize the voice and reading speed?

Absolutely! EchoRead offers extensive customization options. You can select from over 50 different voices spanning various accents and tones. You can also adjust reading speed, add pauses between paragraphs or chapters, and even customize emphasis for specific words or phrases.
