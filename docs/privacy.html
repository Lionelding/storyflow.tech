Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2025

We at Storyflow are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our service to convert eBooks into audiobooks.

Information We Collect

  • Files You Upload: We collect the EPUB or PDF files you upload for conversion.
  • Technical Data: We may collect your IP address to improve our service and troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Your Information

  • Conversion: Your uploaded files are used solely to create your audiobook.
  • Deletion Policy:
    • Uploaded eBooks (EPUB/PDF) are deleted immediately after conversion.
    • Converted audio files are stored for 24 hours to give you time to download them. After 24 hours, they're permanently erased.
  • Service Improvements: Aggregated, anonymized data (e.g., average conversion time) may be used to enhance our platform.

Information Sharing

We do not share your eBooks, audio files, or personal data with third parties. Ever.

Security

  • All data transfers use encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) to protect your files.
  • We employ industry-standard practices to safeguard your data during processing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted here, and the "Last Updated" date will reflect the latest version.

Contact Us

For questions about privacy or data handling, reach us at info@storyflow.com.
